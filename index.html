PSALMS, HYMNS and SPIRITUAL SONGS

Adegboye Samson (Nazarite) is called to minister the necessity of sustaining man to God relationship having turned to Christ, that man should not lose God’s presence in whatever he does and wherever he may be

“But if we walk in the light, as he is in the light, we have fellowship with one another, and the blood of Jesus, his Son, purifies us from all sin.”


- 1 John 1:7

Called to minister spiritual songs that connects and binds man to God the Father, and also turn the heart of as many to our Lord and Redeemer.

“Let the message of Christ dwell among you richly as you teach and admonish one another with all wisdom through psalms, hymns, and songs from the Spirit, singing to God with gratitude in your hearts.”


- Col 3:16

Called to promote proper biblical interpretation, sound biblical teaching, and a continuous check and balance for the African Church, with a strong pointer toward the need for spiritual reawakening.

“To equip his people for works of service, so that the body of Christ may be built up.”


- Ephesians 4:12
