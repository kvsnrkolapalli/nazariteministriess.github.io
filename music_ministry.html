Music Ministry

  1. Hymn Lamp

    2. Hymn Lamp is a monthly hymnal ministration for the Yoruba Christian Community. The production aims at using the hymns for encouragement, prayers, re-dedication, and so on to strengthen our walk with the Lord. The January edition, okan aare is a hymn of encouragement that alludes to 1 Cor 15:58, which drops by the first week of January
    - Click Here

  2. In Your Presence

    3. In Your Presence is an Online Worship Ministration that holds in the first and second half of the year respectively. It is a call for genuine worship of God from the depth of our being, without limitation to denomination, distance, or language. The first half of the year edition Before the Throne comes up by January 15th, 2023 where we will recount the vision of Isaiah as we access the throne of grace with the confidence made available to us by our Lord Jesus Christ
    - Click Here

  3. AWW (As We Worship)

    4. As we worship is a physical worship meeting for the members of the Celestial Church of Christ. It is a special edition where men and women of like mind tarry in His Presence to worship and express the deep need for Spiritual Reawakening. The first edition is set to come up by April 16th, 2023.
